The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

Game introduction:

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild is a 2017 action-adventure game developed by Nintendo EPD for the Wii U and Nintendo Switch. It is the nineteenth main installment in The Legend of Zelda series and is set near the end of the Zelda timeline. The game follows Link as he awakens from a deep slumber in the Shrine of Resurrection, guided by a mysterious voice to discover the fate of the ruined Kingdom of Hyrule. After leaving the Great Plateau, Link is directed to meet the wise Sheikah elder Impa and learn about the Guardians and Divine Beasts, ancient machines created 10,000 years ago to defeat the Calamity Ganon.

Unique gameplay features:

  1. Open World: For the fisrt time in the Zelda universe, explore an open world where you decide what and where to explore
  2. Unique Shrine Challenges: Explore unique and challenging puzzles within the 120 shrines scattered acrost Hyrule.
  3. Shika Slate Abilities: Unlock powerful mechanics that allow you to create bombs freze objects and enemies, magnetically control objects and more.
  4. Save The Kindom of Hyrule: Defeat the Divine beasts and take down Gannon or storm the castle immediately and face a much stronger foe.
